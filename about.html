What this is, and what it is not.

What is should i?

should i? is a simple tool for getting clear, short, morally serious answers to everyday questions.

Why it exists

A lot of bad decisions are not actually confusing. They are just tempting, impulsive, convenient, ego-driven, or dressed up to sound smarter than they are.

This site exists to cut through that.

The goal

The goal is not to replace judgment. The goal is to give users a fast, honest response that feels more like clarity than rationalization.

The tone

should i? aims to be direct, funny when appropriate, and morally grounded without sounding preachy or sterile.

What it is not

This is not therapy, not legal advice, not medical advice, and not financial advice. It is a reflective tool built for everyday decision-making.
